# Chez Chrystelle Operations Manual

This version is focused on store-specific delivery instructions so it can be used as the first knowledge base for the ops chatbot.

## Park Slope Food Coop (Union Street)

- Deliver every day except Sunday.
- Usually 60 to 100 salads per day.
- Arrive before 8:00 AM whenever possible.
- Park as close as possible, but never use any spots reserved for the fire station.
- Use a u-boat if available. If not, use a Hollywood.
- Bags can be stacked only if the bottom bag is completely full: 30 salads.
- Never put a bag with salads directly on the floor. Keep it on the u-boat or Hollywood until empty.
- Use the service entrance and middle-most gate.
- Go through the plastic flap door. The grab-and-go fridge will be directly in front of you.
- If sushi is being stocked, wait.
- If tacos are being stocked, ask if you can jump ahead.
- Use the 3rd and 4th shelves from the top.
- Stack 3 high, 3 deep, and 5 across.
- Standard layout:
  - Top: 4 rows salmon, then 1 row vegan or plain.
  - Below: 4 rows chicken, then 1 row vegan or plain on the right.
- Give invoice to any full-time employee in the receiving room.
- If nobody is there, leave it on the desk with the tape and markers.
- Check the shelf above the desk for extra bags.
- Return the u-boat to the street.

## Key Food 5th Avenue (5th Avenue and Prospect Avenue)

- Usually staffed from 6:30 AM on.
- If early, knock and someone will usually come.
- Bring the bag to the deli counter in the back.
- Unload salads directly onto the large cutting board so we can keep the bag.
- Leave the invoice next to the salads.
- Check for extra bags to the left of the cutting boards, high in the corner on top of the tall refrigerators.
- Contacts:
  - Javier, manager
  - Francesco, ordering

## Windsor Terrace Food Coop

- Deliver every Thursday.
- Usually 16 to 20 salads.
- Only accepts deliveries after 10:00 AM and before 3:00 PM.
- If no one is there, call Harold: +1 (917) 572-4976.
- Bring the bag inside and help unload into one of the refrigerators.
- No physical invoice. Chrystelle handles the invoice over the phone.

## K-Slope (9th Street)
